*** CREATE RESOURCE GROUP ***
az group create --name myResourceGroup --location "North Europe"

*** CREATE SERVICE PLAN ***
az appservice plan create --name myAppServicePlan --resource-group myResourceGroup --sku S1 --is-linux

*** Create a Docker Compose app ***
az webapp create --resource-group myResourceGroup --plan myAppServicePlan --name concertHubApp --multicontainer-config-type compose --multicontainer-config-file docker-compose.yml

*** Access logs ***
https://concertHubApp.scm.azurewebsites.net/api/logs/docker

*** Access the app ***
http://concertHubApp.azurewebsites.net

*** DELETE ALL RESOURCE GROUPS ***
az group delete --name myResourceGroup
